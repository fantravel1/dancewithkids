Skill Assessment

Our skill assessment system is designed to encourage growth, not create competition. Here is how it works:

1

Movement Tracking

Optional camera-based movement analysis compares your child's moves to the tutorial guide. All processing happens on-device for privacy.

2

Positive Feedback

Scores focus on what your child did well, not what they missed. Encouraging messages celebrate effort and improvement over perfection.

3

Adaptive Difficulty

The system automatically suggests routines at the right level based on recent performance, keeping your child challenged but never frustrated.
