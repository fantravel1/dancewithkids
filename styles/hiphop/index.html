Born in the Bronx

Hip hop dance was born in the 1970s in the South Bronx, New York City. African American and Latino communities created a movement culture alongside DJing, MCing, and graffiti art. Early pioneers like DJ Kool Herc, Afrika Bambaataa, and the Rock Steady Crew shaped the foundations.
