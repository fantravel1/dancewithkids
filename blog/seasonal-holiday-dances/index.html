Year-Round Ideas

üéØ Monthly Traditions You Can Start

  • "Dance of the Month": Every month, pick ONE routine to master. Practice it daily. By month's end, you own it.
  • "Family Dance Night": Same day every week. Everyone dances together. No excuses.
  • "Video Progression": Film the same routine monthly. Watch how you improve over a year.
  • "Birthday Dance": On each family member's birthday, they get to pick 3 routines for a mini-party.
  • "Rainy Day Dances": Bad weather? Perfect excuse for longer dance sessions indoors.
  • "Milestone Celebrations": Got an A? Finished a project? Made a friend? Celebrate with a dance.
