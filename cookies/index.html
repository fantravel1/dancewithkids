Cookie Policy

The only cookies here are the digital kind (sorry, no chocolate chip).

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites save on your computer or device when you visit them. Think of them like little sticky notes that help the website remember who you are and what you like. They make your experience smoother by remembering things like your language preference or which difficulty level you last used.

Cookies are standard technology used by almost all websites. They cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your device. Most cookies are automatically deleted after a set period, while others stay until you clear them from your browser.

2. How We Use Cookies

DanceWithKids uses cookies to make our platform work properly, to remember your preferences, and to understand how families use our service so we can improve it. We use the minimum number of cookies necessary and we never use cookies to serve advertisements or track users across other websites.

In line with our commitment to children's safety and COPPA compliance, we take extra care with cookies: we do not use cookies to track children's behavior, we never serve targeted ads, and analytics cookies are used only in aggregate, never linked to individual child profiles. Cookie consent is managed by the parent or guardian account holder.

3. Types of Cookies

DanceWithKids uses three types of cookies. We do not use any advertising or tracking cookies.

4. Managing Cookies

You have full control over which optional cookies DanceWithKids uses. When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner where you can choose to accept or decline optional cookies. You can change your preference at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer of any page.

Most web browsers also allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can view which cookies are stored, delete them individually or all at once, block cookies from specific websites, and set your browser to notify you when a cookie is being set.

Browser Cookie Settings

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

Please note that disabling essential cookies may prevent some parts of DanceWithKids from working correctly.

5. Third-Party Cookies

DanceWithKids uses very limited third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts to display our website's typography. Google may set cookies related to font delivery.
  • Stripe: Our payment processor may set cookies during the checkout process to prevent fraud and process payments securely.

We do not use any advertising networks, social media tracking pixels, or third-party analytics services that track users across other websites. We will never add advertising cookies to DanceWithKids.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. For significant changes, we will notify you through the cookie consent banner and by email to the parent or guardian account holder.
