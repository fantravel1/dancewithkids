Workshop Types

We host four types of workshops, each designed for a different audience and purpose.

Family Dance Party

Live DJ-hosted dance parties for the whole family. High energy, multi-style playlists, and interactive games. Held monthly on Saturdays.

  • All ages welcome
  • 45 minutes
  • Free, registration required

Teacher Training

Professional development workshops for educators. Learn to use dance in PE, brain breaks, and SEL. Certificate of completion provided.

  • Educators and administrators
  • 60 minutes
  • Free, limited spots

Cultural Dance Series

Monthly workshops exploring dance traditions from around the world. Guest instructors share cultural context, history, and authentic movements.

  • Ages 5+ with families
  • 50 minutes
  • Free, registration required

Special Needs Dance

Adaptive workshops led by certified dance therapists. Sensory-friendly environments, chair dance options, and individualized support for children with special needs.

  • All abilities welcome
  • 40 minutes
  • Free, small groups
