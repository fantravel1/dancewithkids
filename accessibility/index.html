Accessibility

Dance is for everyone. So is our platform.

Our Commitment

At DanceWithKids, we believe every child deserves to experience the joy of dance, regardless of ability. Accessibility is not an afterthought for us; it is a core value woven into everything we build and create.

We are committed to ensuring that our website and platform are accessible to people with disabilities, including those who use assistive technologies such as screen readers, voice recognition software, and keyboard-only navigation. We continuously work to improve the accessibility of our platform and welcome your feedback.

Our goal is to provide an inclusive experience where all families can participate, learn, and grow through dance together.

Conformance Status

WCAG 2.1 Level AA

DanceWithKids targets conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. These guidelines, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C), provide a framework for making web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities.

We regularly audit our platform against WCAG 2.1 AA criteria and work with third-party accessibility consultants to identify and resolve issues. Our most recent accessibility audit was conducted in December 2025, and we address findings on a rolling basis as part of our development process.

Accessibility Features

Keyboard Navigation

All interactive elements are fully accessible via keyboard. Skip links, visible focus indicators, and logical tab order allow users to navigate without a mouse. Our video player supports full keyboard control including play, pause, volume, and caption toggling.

Screen Reader Support

We use semantic HTML, ARIA landmarks, and descriptive labels throughout the platform. All images include meaningful alt text, form elements have associated labels, and dynamic content changes are announced to screen readers. We test with NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver.

Color Contrast

Our color palette meets WCAG AA contrast requirements. Text and interactive elements maintain a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text. We avoid conveying information through color alone and all interactive elements have non-color visual indicators.

Captions & Audio Descriptions

All dance tutorial videos include closed captions describing verbal instructions and music cues. We are progressively adding audio descriptions to our video library that narrate visual dance movements for users with visual impairments.

Responsive & Flexible Layout

Our platform adapts to different screen sizes and supports text resizing up to 200% without loss of content or functionality. Users can customize their experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Reduced Motion

We respect the "prefers-reduced-motion" system setting. When enabled, animations and transitions are minimized or removed. Video autoplay is disabled, and users have full control over when media plays.

Known Limitations

We are transparent about the areas where we are still working toward full accessibility:

  • Audio Descriptions: Not all video content has full audio descriptions yet. We are adding them progressively and prioritize the most popular tutorials first.
  • Older Content: Some earlier tutorial videos may have auto-generated captions that are less accurate. We are reviewing and correcting these on an ongoing basis.
  • Complex Interactive Elements: Some gamification features, including the real-time dance scoring system, may not be fully accessible to screen reader users. We are redesigning these components.
  • PDF Documents: Some downloadable resources may not be fully tagged for screen reader accessibility. We are converting these to accessible HTML and tagged PDF formats.

We are actively working on each of these areas and aim to address them in upcoming releases throughout 2026.

Feedback & Contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of DanceWithKids. If you encounter any barriers, have suggestions for improvement, or need assistance accessing any part of our platform, please do not hesitate to reach out. Your input helps us create a better experience for everyone.

Email: accessibility@dancewithkids.com

Phone: +1 (415) 555-0199 (Monday–Friday, 9 AM–5 PM PT)

Mail: DanceWithKids Accessibility Team, 123 Dance Avenue, Suite 400, San Francisco, CA 94102

Response Time: We aim to acknowledge reports within 2 business days and resolve issues within 10 business days.

When reporting an issue, it is helpful to include the page URL, a description of the problem, the assistive technology you are using (if applicable), and your browser and operating system.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may file a complaint with the relevant regulatory authority in your jurisdiction.

Third-Party Content

Our platform may include content from third-party sources, such as embedded videos, social media widgets, or external links. While we strive to work with partners who share our commitment to accessibility, we cannot guarantee that all third-party content meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards.

If you experience an accessibility issue with any third-party content on our platform, please let us know so we can work with the content provider to find a solution or offer an accessible alternative.
