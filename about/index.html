Our Story

Building the world's safest dance platform for kids, one routine at a time.

Our Mission

We believe every child deserves to experience the joy, confidence, and physical benefits of dance in a safe, supportive environment.

DanceWithKids was born from a simple observation: millions of kids were missing out on dance because of safety concerns, lack of access, or anxiety about traditional dance classes.

Our mission is to democratize dance education, making it accessible, safe, and fun for families everywhere.
