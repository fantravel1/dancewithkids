Our Commitment

At DanceWithKids, we celebrate all families and all identities. Dance is for everyone. Always.

  • LGBTQ+ families are whole families, worthy of celebration and respect.

Why This Matters for Dance

Dance & Gender Expression

Dance has historically been sorted by gender: "boy dances" and "girl dances." This is limiting and outdated.

Our approach: All dance styles are for all bodies and identities. A girl can dance hip hop powerfully. A boy can do a lyrical, emotional routine. A non-binary kid can be fierce or gentle or both. Movement is personal expression, not gender expression.

Dance as Identity Safety

For LGBTQ+ kids, finding spaces where they're fully accepted is crucial.

DanceWithKids offers: A place where identity is celebrated, never questioned. Where pronouns are respected. Where all family structures are normalized. Where authentic self-expression is the point.

Dance as Safe Self-Expression

For many LGBTQ+ kids, dance becomes a place to express their authentic self. In a world that might not accept them, dance is freedom.

  • No Gender Pressure: Dance how you feel, not how your body is "supposed" to move.
  • Pure Expression: Style choices (movement, music, costumes) are purely personal. No judgment.
  • Body Autonomy: Your body is yours. You decide how much or little to show, how to move, what feels safe.
  • Community: You're not alone. Many LGBTQ+ kids find community and belonging through dance.
  • Confidence: When you're celebrated as you are, confidence follows. That changes everything.
