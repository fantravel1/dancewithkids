Accessibility Features

üëÅÔ∏è Visual Accessibility

  • Closed Captions (CC): Every routine video includes captions for dialogue and music cues
  • Audio Descriptions: Key visual cues are described verbally for blind/low-vision users
  • High Contrast Mode: Website supports high contrast themes for users with low vision
  • Adjustable Text Size: Website text can be enlarged to 200% without loss of functionality
  • Color Accessibility: Videos avoid color-only information; we use patterns and labels

üéß Audio Accessibility

  • Subtitles & Captions: All audio content is captioned
  • Volume Control: Easy audio level adjustment in player settings
  • Transcript Available: Full transcripts for all instructional content
  • Background Music Options: Ability to mute music and dance to silence or background sound

üß† Cognitive & Learning Accessibility

  • Clear Instructions: Routines are taught step-by-step with simple, clear language
  • Repetition: Every move is shown multiple times before combination
  • Variable Playback Speed: Slow down, speed up, or loop specific sections
  • Visual Counting: On-screen beat counting helps with rhythm tracking
  • Memory-Friendly: Routines are structured for easier memorization

ü§∏ Physical & Mobility Accessibility

  • Chair Dance Options: Routines adapted for seated dancers (full-body, not just arms)
  • Modified Movements: Alternatives for high-impact, spinning, or balance-heavy moves
  • One-Sided Adaptations: Routines available for right-side or left-side limited mobility
  • Partner Variations: Options for movements done with physical support
  • Low-Impact Alternatives: Dancing without jumping or high-impact moves

Sensory & Neurodivergent Friendly Features

üé® Sensory-Friendly Mode

  • Reduced Visual Stimulation: Lower saturation, calm color palettes available
  • Instrumental-Only Versions: No lyrics, pure music for reduced auditory input
  • Slower Tempo Options: Less overwhelming pace and rhythm
  • Simplified Choreography: Fewer movements, clearer sequences, repetitive patterns
  • Predictable Structure: Same opening, structure, and closing every time

üß© Neurodivergent-Friendly Design

  • Clear Instructions: Explicit, step-by-step, no assumptions about prior knowledge
  • Consistent Formatting: Every routine has the same structure (warm-up, routine, cool-down)
  • Pause & Control: Full pause/play/rewind control. Watch at own pace.
  • No Time Pressure: Can repeat routines as many times as needed
  • Anxiety-Aware: No mention of "performance" or "doing it right" ‚Äî just movement
