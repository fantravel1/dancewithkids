Expert-Reviewed Content Only

Every routine passes through our multi-stage review by child development experts before publication.

Content Review Process

Every routine goes through a rigorous five-step review before it reaches your child.

1

Choreographer Submission

All choreographers are vetted professionals with background checks and child safety training certifications. They submit routines with detailed documentation of target age, movements used, and learning objectives.

2

Physical Safety Review

A pediatric physical therapist evaluates every movement for age-appropriateness, joint safety, and injury risk. Movements that could strain developing bodies are modified or removed.

3

Child Development Check

Our child psychologist reviews the emotional tone, language, pacing, and instructional approach to ensure it supports positive self-esteem and developmental milestones.

4

Modesty and Appropriateness Audit

All costumes, camera angles, and movement styles are audited to ensure they meet our strict modesty guidelines. We follow international best practices for child representation in media.

5

Family Beta Test

Before public release, every routine is tested with real families from our beta community. Parents provide feedback on safety, fun factor, and age-appropriateness. Only routines that pass all five stages are published.

Privacy & Data Protection

We go beyond legal requirements to protect your family's privacy.

COPPA Compliant

We fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act. Parental consent is required for all account creation, and children's data is never collected, stored, or shared.

GDPR Ready

European families are protected under GDPR with full data portability, right to deletion, and transparent data processing. We maintain servers in multiple regions for compliance.

No Third-Party Tracking

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or share data with advertisers. Analytics are first-party and anonymized. We never build profiles on children.

Encrypted and Secure

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security protocols, conduct regular security audits, and maintain a responsible disclosure program.
