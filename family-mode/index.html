Family Mode

Because the best dances happen when the whole family joins in!

What is Family Mode?

Family Mode transforms DanceWithKids into a multi-player dance experience where parents, siblings, and grandparents can all dance together on one screen. It automatically adjusts difficulty for each participant so everyone has fun, regardless of age or skill level.

Whether it is a rainy Saturday afternoon or a post-dinner dance party, Family Mode turns screen time into quality time that gets your whole family moving, laughing, and bonding through dance.
