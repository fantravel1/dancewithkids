? General Questions

What age group is DanceWithKids designed for?+
DanceWithKids is designed for children ages 3 to 12 and their families. Our Easy level suits ages 3-6 with simple movements and short durations. Moderate is ideal for ages 6-10 with coordination challenges and longer sequences. Advanced challenges dancers ages 9 and up with full choreography and performance skills. Parents and caregivers are always encouraged to dance along at every level.
Do I need dance experience to use this platform?+
Not at all! DanceWithKids is built for absolute beginners. Our Easy level starts with simple movements like clapping, swaying, and marching that anyone can follow. Every routine includes a follow-along mode with on-screen cues. No prior dance training is needed for kids or parents.
How are the difficulty levels structured?+
We have three progressive levels. Easy features 1-2 minute routines with repetitive, simple movements for ages 3-6. Moderate offers 3-5 minute routines with coordination combos and rhythm changes for ages 6-10. Advanced provides 4-8 minute performance-ready choreography with complex sequences for ages 9 and up. Children can progress at their own pace, and there is no pressure to advance.
What dance styles do you offer?+
We offer routines across seven dance styles: Pop, Hip Hop, Afrobeat, Latin, K-Pop, Contemporary, and Freestyle. Each style is taught with cultural respect and age-appropriate movements. We regularly add new styles and routines based on community feedback and global dance trends.
Can siblings of different ages use the same account?+
Yes! A single family account supports multiple child profiles, each with their own difficulty level, progress tracking, and routine recommendations. Our Family Mode even lets siblings dance together with routines designed for mixed skill levels so everyone can join in.

How do you ensure child safety on the platform?+
Child safety is our top priority. All content is reviewed by child development experts before publication. We collect no personal data from children and comply fully with COPPA regulations. There are no social features that allow direct contact between users. All routines are age-appropriate and modesty-conscious. Our safety team reviews content daily, and we maintain a zero-tolerance policy for anything that could compromise child wellbeing.
Do you collect data from children?+
No. We do not collect personal information from children under 13. Parent accounts handle all data management. Child profiles use only a first name or nickname chosen by the parent. We never use cookies to track children, and we do not show advertisements to minors. Our full privacy policy is available for review at any time.
Are the dance movements physically safe for children?+
Every routine is designed with input from pediatric physical therapists and child development specialists. Movements are age-appropriate and avoid anything that could strain developing joints or muscles. Easy routines include only gross motor movements. Moderate and Advanced routines include built-in warm-ups and cool-downs. We also provide safety tips for setting up a safe dance space at home.
How can I report a safety concern?+
You can report any safety concern through our dedicated safety reporting form on the Safety page, by emailing safety@dancewithkids.com, or by using the report button available on every routine page. Safety reports are reviewed within 2 hours during business hours and within 12 hours outside business hours. All reports are treated with the highest priority and confidentiality.

üíª Technical Questions

What devices and browsers are supported?+
DanceWithKids works on any modern web browser including Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge. We support smartphones, tablets, laptops, desktop computers, and smart TVs with a built-in browser. For the best experience on mobile, download our free app available on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play). We recommend a screen size of at least 7 inches for the optimal follow-along experience.
Can I download routines for offline use?+
Premium subscribers can download up to 25 routines at a time for offline use through our mobile app. Downloads are available on iOS and Android devices. This is perfect for road trips, areas with limited internet, or outdoor dance sessions. Downloaded routines remain available for 30 days and automatically refresh when you reconnect to the internet.
The video is buffering or not loading. What should I do?+
First, check your internet connection speed. We recommend at least 5 Mbps for smooth playback. Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or switching to a different browser. If using the mobile app, make sure it is updated to the latest version. You can also try lowering the video quality in the player settings. If the problem persists, contact our support team at hello@dancewithkids.com with your device type and browser version.
How do I cast routines to my TV?+
You can cast from your phone or tablet to your TV using Chromecast, AirPlay, or any screen mirroring feature built into your device. On smart TVs, you can also access DanceWithKids directly through the built-in web browser. For the best big-screen experience, connect your laptop to your TV via HDMI. Dancing in front of a large screen makes it much easier for young children to follow along.

Is DanceWithKids free to use?+
Yes! Our core platform with dozens of routines across all difficulty levels is completely free forever. We believe every child should have access to the joy of dance. Premium subscriptions unlock additional routines, offline downloads, advanced progress tracking, family accounts with multiple profiles, and exclusive monthly challenges starting at $7.99 per month or $59.99 per year.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?+
Absolutely. You can cancel your premium subscription at any time with no cancellation fees or penalties. Simply go to your account settings and select Cancel Subscription. You will retain access to all premium features until the end of your current billing period. After cancellation, your account reverts to our free tier with full access to all free routines. Your progress and history are never deleted.
Do you offer discounts for schools or nonprofits?+
Yes, we offer special pricing for educational institutions, after-school programs, community centers, and registered nonprofits. School licenses include classroom management tools, progress reports for educators, and bulk account setup. Contact our partnerships team at partners@dancewithkids.com for a custom quote based on your organization's needs and number of students.
