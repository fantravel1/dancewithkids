How Challenges Work

Dance challenges are themed activities that encourage kids to practice, explore new styles, and celebrate their progress. Every challenge is designed to be fun first, with no rankings or public comparisons. Everyone who participates is a winner!

Challenge Categories

W

Weekly Challenges

Quick, fun challenges that refresh every week. Perfect for building a regular dance habit. Typically take 15 to 30 minutes to complete.

M

Monthly Challenges

Deeper exploration of a theme or dance style over 4 weeks. Multiple milestones and a special badge for completion. Great for building new skills.

S

Seasonal Challenges

Epic 3-month challenges tied to seasons and holidays. The biggest badges, the most milestones, and the most rewarding sense of accomplishment.

Create Your Own Challenge

Premium families can create custom challenges for their household. Set the theme, choose the routines, and design your own family badge. It is a wonderful way to make dance practice exciting and personal.

Safety in Challenges

All challenges on DanceWithKids are designed with child safety in mind. There are no public leaderboards showing individual children. Challenges focus on personal improvement and participation rather than competition. Children never upload videos or photos. All participation data is visible only to the parent or guardian through the Parent Dashboard.
