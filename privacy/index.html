Privacy Policy

Your family's privacy is our top priority.

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

DanceWithKids ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families who use our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services. Please read this policy carefully. By accessing or using DanceWithKids, you agree to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, and password. For parent or guardian accounts, we collect the parent's name, email address, and billing information if you subscribe to a paid plan. For child profiles, we collect only a display name (which may be a nickname) and age range to personalize the experience.

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our platform, we automatically collect certain technical information, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, device type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs. We use this information to improve our service, ensure security, and analyze usage patterns.

Usage Data

We collect data about how your child interacts with our platform, including dance routines completed, skill progress, badges earned, and session duration. This data is used solely to personalize the learning experience and provide progress reports to parents.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our dance education platform
  • To personalize content and dance routines based on skill level and age
  • To process subscriptions and payments securely
  • To communicate with parents about account activity, updates, and support requests
  • To generate progress reports and skill assessments for parents
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats
  • To comply with legal obligations, including child safety regulations
  • To conduct research and analysis to improve our educational content

We do not use children's personal information for advertising or marketing purposes. We do not sell personal information to third parties under any circumstances.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

DanceWithKids is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We take extra care to protect children under 13 and apply these protections to all young users on our platform.

  • Verifiable Parental Consent: We require verifiable parental consent before collecting any personal information from children under 13. Parents must create the account and explicitly authorize their child's use of the platform.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service. Child profiles require only a display name and age range.
  • No Behavioral Advertising: We never serve targeted or behavioral advertising to children. Our platform is ad-free for all users.
  • Parental Control: Parents can review, modify, or delete their child's information at any time through the Parent Dashboard. Parents may also revoke consent and request complete deletion of their child's data.
  • No Public Profiles: Children's profiles are never publicly visible. All data is accessible only to the parent or guardian who created the account.
  • Safe Communication: Children cannot communicate with other users, share personal information, or post content publicly on our platform.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will delete that information promptly.

4. Data Storage & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Strict access controls and authentication protocols
  • Secure data centers located in the United States and European Union
  • Passwords stored using strong, one-way hashing algorithms

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. When you delete your account, we remove your personal information from our active systems within 30 days. Some data may be retained in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before permanent deletion.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to promptly notifying affected users in the event of a data breach.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We use limited analytics cookies to understand how families use our platform so we can improve the experience. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. For a detailed overview of each cookie we use and how to manage them, please see our Cookie Policy.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings or through the cookie consent banner on our website. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our platform.

6. Third-Party Services

We work with a limited number of trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our platform. These include:

  • Payment Processors (Stripe): For handling subscription payments securely
  • Cloud Hosting (AWS): For securely storing and serving data
  • Analytics Services: Privacy-focused, COPPA-compliant tools for understanding usage patterns
  • Email Service Providers: For sending account notifications to parents only

All third-party providers are contractually required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your data. They may only use your information as directed by us and for the purposes specified in their service agreements. We never sell or share personal data with third parties for commercial or advertising purposes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about your family
  • Correction: Update or correct any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your family's data from our systems
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable, machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we process your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent for data processing at any time

For residents of the European Economic Area (EEA), we process data under the lawful bases of consent, contract performance, and legitimate interests and comply fully with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For California residents, we comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below. We will respond to verified requests within 30 days.

8. Contact for Privacy Concerns

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our Privacy Team:

Email: privacy@dancewithkids.com

Mail: DanceWithKids Privacy Team, 123 Dance Avenue, Suite 400, San Francisco, CA 94102

Response Time: We aim to respond to all inquiries within 5 business days.

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and, where appropriate, by sending an email notification to the address associated with your account. Your continued use of DanceWithKids after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
